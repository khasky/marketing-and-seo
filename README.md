# Marketing and SEO: a practical guide

Linked Markdown guides for founders, marketers, and product teams. Topics run from research and legal basics to acquisition, content, SEO, and paid media.

## Who this is for

- Early-stage SaaS and product teams planning launch, landing structure, and basic legal coverage.
- Marketers building organic and community-led acquisition (email, social, directories, comparisons).
- Founders who want one path from idea validation through retention and remarketing.
